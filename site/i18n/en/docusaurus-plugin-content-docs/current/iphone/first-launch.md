---
title: What to Check on First Launch
description: This page explains the permissions and settings worth checking when you launch the app for the first time.
---

On first launch, it is enough to set up the permissions and display options that help you avoid confusion later. You do not need to recreate the Mac-side environment from the iPhone.

## Permissions you are likely to see first

- notifications
  Useful when you want to notice that a thread finished while you are away from the desk.
- camera
  Used when you scan the Mac pairing QR from `Add from QR`.
- local network
  Used when the iPhone finds and connects to the Bridge on the same network.

Camera matters when you use QR. Local network access matters when you test the connection and import projects after adding a Host. You can always allow them later, but granting them early keeps the flow from stopping.

## Settings that improve readability early

You can change these in `Settings`:

- `Font Size`
  Worth adjusting early if you often read long logs or `Work Log` output.
- `Theme`
  Useful when you move between bright and dark places.
- `Follow-up behavior`
  Lets you choose whether the default is `Queue` or `Steer`.
- `KeepAlive` and `Timeout`
  Useful when you want to tune behavior on unstable connections.

For most people, changing only `Font Size` at the beginning is enough.

## Things that are still normal at this stage

- there are zero Hosts
- there are zero Projects
- there are no Threads yet

From here, go to `Hosts` and import the Mac.
